Other OSS Packages Distributed with STAF

Other Open Source Software (OSS) packages are distributed as part of STAF (or its downloadable services), but are licensed under their own licenses and are not subject to STAF's EPL license.

  • zLib is distributed as part of STAF and is used by the ZIP service
  • OpenSSL is distributed as part of STAF and is used by the STAF Secure TCP Connection Provider and by the FS service for performing checksums
  • Xerces2-J is distributed with the STAX and HTTP services
  • Xalan-J is distributed with the STAX service
  • Jython is distributed with the STAX, Cron and EventManager services
  • Commons Logging, Commons Codec, Commons HttpClient, and CyberNeko HTML Parser are distributed with the HTTP service
